Imagine a fat, crisp but soft within, juicy jalebi, each one more than 250 grams, fried in pure Desi Ghee. At Jai Bhole Jalebi Bhandar, king size jalebis which are affectionately called Jalebas, are served hot off the girdle after being soaked in a saffron-flavoured sugar syrup. The size of a single jaleba prepared by Jai bhole can go upto 500 grams. Best had piping hot, the succulent Jaleba is a must try original at the Jai Bhole Jalebi Bhandaar.
